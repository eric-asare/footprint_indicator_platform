Why are you here?

The imagining of possibilities to address climate change is not only the job of politicians or decision makers in closed door rooms but also you and me!

What is the problem we are trying to solve?

(1) No transperancy on how online calculators compute carbon footprint

(2) Computation are offered based on data from the Global North

How is Footprint Indicator Platform any different?

* Is transparent, all the code on github linked below

* Computation specifically tailored to the UAE (considered Global South)

* Shows how much various sectors contribute to co2 emissions

* Questions the lifestyle of the people

Our goals for you

*Get to know your footprint by taking the lifestyle quiz

* Understand your footprint results through visualizations

* Download your results for later

* Take action now by pledging a lifestyle change

* Learn more about climate efforts and lifestyle changes by visiting the learn more tab

* Be motivated to start something new with your skills towards Net Zero!

* Contribute to this project as a designer, environmentalist, programmer - all are welcome - github

* Share the link to this site with your friends

The Process

Over 100hours and 1000 lines of code went into this project!

The steps involved:

* Refining the idea

* Making sketches of the platform

* Desigining the lifestyle questions

* Developing the computation for calculating the footprint

* Building visualizations to help you understand your carbon footprint results

* Adding a pledge page powered by a persistent database and an API server so you can take an action now

* Adding more resources on the learn more tab for you

* Creating this page so you understand what the project is about
